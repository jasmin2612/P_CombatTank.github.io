Combat Tanks
About game:
  • A fun and engaging 2D tank battle game designed specifically for children, offering simple controls and exciting battles against AI opponents.
  • Features bright, colorful visuals and child-friendly designs to create an appealing and approachable gaming experience for younger players.
  • Includes multiple levels with interactive power-ups and creative layouts, encouraging exploration and replayability while maintaining a sense of fun and adventure.
  • Offers adjustable difficulty settings, ensuring a balanced experience that caters to children of various age groups and skill levels.
My Work:
  • Conceptualized and developed the game, focusing on delivering an enjoyable and educational experience tailored to a younger audience.
  • Created adaptive AI opponents that adjust their behavior based on the chosen difficulty, providing a challenge that is neither too easy nor frustrating for children.
  • Designed an intuitive tank selection system with diverse options, making it easy for players to experiment with different tanks while promoting creative play.
  • Developed visually appealing and child-friendly levels with interactive elements and strategically placed power-ups to keep players engaged.
  • Conducted thorough playtesting to ensure smooth performance, age-appropriate content, and an enjoyable user experience across all devices.
